About

Obianuju is a Design Engineer who bridges the worlds of design and technology to build products that are as intuitive as they are impactful. With a background in product design and a growing expertise in front-end engineering, she brings ideas to life through seamless user experiences and clean, scalable code.

She has worked on B2B platforms across logistics, hospitality, and e-commerce — designing customer dashboards, building AI-powered chatbot experiences, and shaping interfaces that simplify complexity for real people. Her approach blends visual storytelling with technical precision, ensuring that every product not only looks good but also works beautifully.

When she’s not deep in Figma or refining CSS grids, you’ll find her exploring how emerging tools — from motion design platforms like Jitter to AI integrations — can push the boundaries of digital experiences.

In her spare time, she spends most of it either with friends or family, binge watching her favorite TV shows, reading the same book she has been on for the past year, or just serving in her local church.
